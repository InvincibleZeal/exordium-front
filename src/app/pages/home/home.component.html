What is Exordium?

Exordium is a technology event hosted by the National Institute of Technology, Srinagar. It hosts a variety of events that include competitions, exhibitions, lectures and workshops. The aim of Exordium is to provide a platform for students to develop and showcase their technical prowess. Exordium is Kashmir's largest and only event of its kind.

Exordium will commence from the 3rd of April and continue till 7th of April. In this span, multiple events and competitions will be conducted in the institute campus. Most of these events are aimed at helping the students develop their technical skills in a competitive environment. A few non technical events, such as art and photography based events, will be conducted in between so as to allow college students from all branches to participate irrespective of their technical knowledge levels.
